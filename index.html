I am a master's student in mathematics and advised by Mohan Bhupal.

My research interests are mainly in low dimensional topology. To be more precise, trisections of smooth 4-manifolds and knot theory are where I spend most of my time. My master's thesis focuses on trisections of 4-manifolds.
Prior to my current research, in undergraduate completion thesis, I did my research on classical groups.
